KFC just made an official Colonel Sanders Dating Sim. Yes, this is real life.

By Thomas | 25th Sept 2019

Yes. This is apparently a real, true to God creation - I Love You, Colonel Sanders! A Finger Lickin’ Good Dating Simulator. And what a game this is. Finally, we're given the opportunity of a lifetime: the chance to romance fast food's grand daddy, the Colonel himself. As you make your way through this authentic Japanese-style dating simulator, you'll encounter a cast of colourful and romantic chefs. Take your pick!

The entire game takes place inside an ordinary-looking college in middle America. You, a culinary student, must try your absolute hardest to entice your handsome classmate, the Colonel. All the stops will be pulled out - you must let nothing get in the way of you and the Colonel of your dreams. Don't let this golden opportunity go to waste!

I Love You, Colonel Sanders! A Finger Lickin’ Good Dating Simulator is now available on Steam for free. Console releases have not been confirmed.
